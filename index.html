Majek Fashek
(1963-2020)

The legend: Majek Fashek


Majek Fashek, born Majekodunmi Fasheke in 1963 was a Nigerian singer who achieved international acclaim in 1988 for his album, prisoner of conscience.

Shortly after that he was signed to interscope records where his contract was terminated due to his excessive drug addiction.

However, he is best remembered for his legendary contribution to Afro pop and raggae music.

He released many classic songs like "Send Down the Rain" and "Holy Spirit"
